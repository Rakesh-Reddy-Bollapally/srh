Team Overview


Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a professional cricket franchise based in Hyderabad, Telangana, that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2012, SRH replaced the Deccan Chargers, who were terminated from the IPL following the 2012 season. The team is owned by the Sun TV Network and has since become one of the most competitive teams in the league.

Home Ground Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium
Champions 1 (2016)
Founded 2012
Owner Sun TV Network
Present Captain Pat Cummins
Notable Players David Warner, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Rashid Khan, Shikhar Dhawan
